== Installation ==
WordPress versions: 4.4.0 and up.

Step-by-step Guide:

- <strong>Step 1:</strong> Navigate to your WordPress Plugin Directory to install Resolution1 (R1) Contact Widget plugin and activate it on your WordPress website. ([Read More](https://wordpress.org/support/article/managing-plugins/#automatic-plugin-installation));
<p></p>
- <strong>Step 2:</strong> Go to Settings > Resolution1 (R1) Contact Widget > Connect with Resolution1;
<p></p>
- <strong>Step 3:</strong> Add login credentials to get Resolution1 (R1) Token.
<p></p>
- <strong>Step 4:</strong> Login/SignUp to your Resolution1 (R1) Dashboard ([Resolution1.com](https://dashboard.resolution1.com/login));
<p></p>
- <strong>Step 5:</strong> Go to Website Widgets > Resolution1 (R1) Contact Widget;
<p></p>
- <strong>Step 6:</strong> Customize and Save the Resolution1 (R1) Contact Widget to fit your website style and design;
<p></p>
- <strong>Step 7:</strong> Navigate back to WordPress Admin Panel and go to Appearance > Widgets; and
<p></p>
- <strong>Step 8:</strong> Finally display your Resolution1 (R1) Contact Widget on your website by selecting the Resolution1 (R1) Contact Widget from your Available Widgets and adding it to the Footer of your website.
